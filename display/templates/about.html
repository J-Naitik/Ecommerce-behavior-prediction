How Our Recommendation Engine Works

Item-Based Collaborative Filtering

Our recommendation engine uses advanced machine learning techniques to analyze your purchase history and find products similar to ones you've already bought.

Similarity Scoring

We calculate similarity scores between products using cosine similarity on a user-item purchase matrix. Products with higher similarity scores are more likely to match your preferences.

Personalized Recommendations

Each recommendation is tailored to your unique purchase history. We weight recent and frequently purchased items more heavily to ensure relevant suggestions.

Continuous Improvement

Our system learns from patterns in millions of transactions to continuously improve recommendation accuracy and help you discover products you'll love.

Key Metrics

  • Similarity Score: Ranges from 0 to 1, showing how closely a product relates to your past purchases
  • Match Percentage: Displayed as a percentage for easy understanding
  • Top Recommendations: We show your top 5 most relevant products
